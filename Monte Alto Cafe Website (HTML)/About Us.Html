About Us

 

Great Coffee


It's in our genes. We are committed to bringing the best coffee to our customers at a fair price. Premium Estate Coffee from quality plantations.


Three Generations Of Excellence


Our family has been in the coffee business for three generations. We represent premium growers in the region.


Our Heritage


In 1928 three young brothers left their native Germany and started a coffee plantation in South America. Today, this premium shade plantation has won awards.

Cafe Monte Alto was established in 1997 when we began importing premium green coffee beans from family-owned farms.


Our commitment to you


Environment


We are committed to producing premium quality coffee in harmony with the environment. All of our coffee is 100% shade-grown Arabica.
