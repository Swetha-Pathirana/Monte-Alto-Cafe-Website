Cafe


Open Daily From 6 To 6

If you're looking for a great cup of coffee and a warm, inviting atmosphere, you've come to the right place!

When you walk into the cafe, you will be greeted by our friendly staff and an exceptional selection of coffees and teas prepared to order by our well-trained baristas.

The cafe walls are adorned with beautiful artwork by local artists and offer plenty of seating.

A perfect place for getting together with friends or finding a quiet nook to enjoy a cup of premium coffee and a good read.

We've been in the business of creating great coffee for over 65 years. All of our coffee is 100% Arabica, 100% Shade Grown, and 100% Fair Trade.

We are committed to producing premium quality coffee in harmony with the environment and to fair pricing at each level in the product chain, sustaining the farmers so the availability of fine specialty coffee can continue and providing our customers with good quality and value.

We are located in Plymouth, New Hampshire, a small but wonderful community that loves great coffee. Stop by for a cup!
